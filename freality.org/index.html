"free" + "reality", freality is an experiment.

The word "reality" often carries a connotation of the hard truth. "Reality" as opposed to ideal. "Reality" as opposed to dreams. If this dichotomy is accepted, daily life becomes dark and burdensome; dreams an escape.

The word "free" also commonly exists within this perspective. If we know of freedom, it is freedom from something, freedom from our burdens. Freedom as the opposite of some bad condition.

But recently (at least within the small part of the world this page inhabits), the word "free" has begun to rekindle its positive meaning, particularly in the context of the technical culture that supports and advocates this global public internet. Among these people, "free" describes more than the absence of burden. Rather, it is more commonly used to describe the opportunity for cooperation; the ease and fun of sharing :)

Freedom, so understood, modifies the perspective of daily life. Freedom, as fun, makes reality a wonderful truth. Not a place to escape from in dreams, but a place to live in, awake.

The word "freality" is a simple little meme, with one purpose. To enter the mind and in an easy little thought restore this way of thinking.

Here, you will find experiments in freality. A library, a laboratory, and a small community. Free music and literature. Honest politics and philosophy. Art. Science. And hopefully some thinking about our spirit, what makes us human, and where we belong in this cosmos.
