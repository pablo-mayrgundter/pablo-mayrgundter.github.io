Vote Nader

"We need a world where politics is activated by vision, not fear, where media is created by people, not corporations, where energy is produced more from the sun and less from oil, where business is driven by human need, not greed, and where foreign policy is dedicated to justice -- not war. No Justice, No Peace." -- Ralph Nader

Ralph Nader is on the ballot of 34 states at the time of this writing. He is running a national presidential campaign without the massive monies of the RDNC candidates. He has his facts straight and sets a new standard for a "3rd Party" candidate. He's debated the RDNC candidates on the web, and shown a clear and comprehensive understanding of the problems facing this country and how to best solve them.

If you're tired of postponing your ideals for yet another election of RDNC candidates, if you think the lesser of two evils still leads us down the wrong path, consider a vote for Nader.
